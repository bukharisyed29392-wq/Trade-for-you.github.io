Trading Strategies

Short practical strategies with example setups and risk rules.

1. Trend Following

Look for higher highs and higher lows in uptrends. Trade pullbacks to the moving average with a stop under the recent swing low.

Risk: 1-2% per trade. Use proper position sizing.

2. Scalping

Short timeframes (1–15min). Tight stops and quick exits. Requires low spreads and fast execution.

3. Swing Trading

Hold trades for days to weeks. Focus on daily charts and key support/resistance zones.

Risk Management

Define stop-loss, calculate position size before entering, diversify trades, and keep a trading journal.
