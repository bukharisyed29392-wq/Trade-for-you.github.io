Forex Basics

Start here if you are new. Short clear explanations with examples.

What is Forex?

Forex (foreign exchange) is the global market for trading currencies. Traders buy and sell currency pairs like EUR/USD to profit from price changes.

Pips & Lots

Pip: smallest price move in a currency pair. Lot: standard trade size (1 lot = 100,000 units). Example calculations are shown here for clarity.

Leverage & Margin

Leverage lets you control a larger position with small capital. It increases both profits and losses. Learn to use it responsibly and always use stop-loss.

Order Types

Market order, limit order, stop order — know the difference and when to use each to manage entries and exits.
