Forex Trading Basics

Understand the fundamentals of forex trading to start your journey.

Currency Pairs

Trade one currency against another (e.g., EUR/USD = Euro vs. US Dollar). Major pairs like USD/JPY are most liquid.

Pips

A pip is the smallest price move in forex (e.g., 0.0001 in EUR/USD). It measures your profit or loss.

Leverage

Leverage lets you trade larger amounts with less money (e.g., 1:100). It increases both profits and risks.

Tip: Start with low leverage (1:10) on brokers like Exness or XM.
