Forex Trading Strategies for Beginners

Discover simple strategies to trade forex like a pro.

1. Scalping

Scalping involves making quick, small trades (minutes or seconds) to profit from tiny price movements. Use 1-minute or 5-minute charts and focus on liquid pairs like EUR/USD.

Tip: Use low spreads (e.g., with Exness) and set tight stop-losses.

2. Trend Following

Trade in the direction of the market trend (up or down) using moving averages (e.g., 50-day or 200-day). Buy when prices rise, sell when they fall.

3. RSI Strategy

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures if a currency is overbought (above 70) or oversold (below 30). Buy when oversold, sell when overbought.

Tip: Combine RSI with support/resistance levels for better trades.
