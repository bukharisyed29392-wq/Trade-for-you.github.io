Forex Trading Strategies

Master beginner-friendly strategies to trade forex like a pro.

Scalping

Scalping involves quick trades (minutes or seconds) to profit from small price movements. Use 1-minute or 5-minute charts on pairs like EUR/USD.

Tip: Choose brokers with low spreads like Exness.

Trend Following

Trade in the direction of market trends using moving averages (e.g., 50-day). Buy in uptrends, sell in downtrends.

RSI Strategy

The Relative Strength Index (RSI) identifies overbought (above 70) or oversold (below 30) conditions. Buy when oversold, sell when overbought.
